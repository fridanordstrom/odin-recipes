Semla

Description

A semla is a delicious Swedish cardamom-scented, buttery bun filled with sweet almond paste and fluffy whipped cream. Semlor buns are traditionally eaten the day before the start of Lent, but they are so enjoyable that I suggest you make them anytime!

Ingredients

  • 12 semla buns
  • 14 ounces almond paste
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar

Steps

  1. Combine almond paste, powdered sugar, milk, and cardamom in a food processor and blend almond filling until smooth.
  2. Cut a triangle from the top of the buns, and remove some of the crumbs to make room for the filling. Pipe almond filling into each bun, and top with whipped cream. Place the triangle on top and dust with powdered sugar before serving.
